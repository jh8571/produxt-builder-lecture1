Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Introduction

At AI Life Insights, we prioritize your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle information when you use our website and AI tools.

Data Collection

AI Tools: Our "Animal Face Classification" tool runs entirely in your browser using TensorFlow.js. Your images are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services.

Log Files: Like most websites, we collect non-identifiable information such as IP addresses, browser types, and referring pages to analyze trends and improve site performance.

Cookies and Web Beacons

We use cookies to store your preferences (such as dark/light mode). Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Ads Settings.

Contact Us
